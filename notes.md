# Dataset Options
[mcPhase](https://physionet.org/content/mcphases/1.0.0/) 
- It is a big dataset, will take up some space on our own computer. 3.4GB uncompressed.
- Variances: exercise, glucose, height and weight, temperature, sleep, hormone (phase of menstruation)
- We can try to predict stress score based on exercise, temperature, phase
- Li: it contains self-reported symptoms (e.g. menstrual cycle phase, flow volume, color, appetite, stress, fatigue, etc.)

[Lifestyle and sleep patterns](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/minahilfatima12328/lifestyle-and-sleep-patterns)
- See what occupation, age, sex, exercise link to sleep disorder (often use logistic regression)

[Sleep Efficiency Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/equilibriumm/sleep-efficiency/data)
- This included caffeine consumption, alcohol consumption, exercise, smoking, gender, age, and sleep duration, sleep efficiency
